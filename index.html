Academic Overview

Our school offers a comprehensive and rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in college and beyond.

Academic Programs

  • Primary Education (Grades K-5)

    Our primary education program focuses on building a strong foundation in reading, writing, mathematics, and science.

  • Middle School (Grades 6-8)

    Our middle school program provides a challenging and supportive environment that prepares students for high school.

  • High School (Grades 9-12)

    Our high school program offers a wide range of courses, including Advanced Placement and honors classes.

Curriculum

  • Core Subjects

    English, mathematics, science, social studies, and foreign languages.

  • Electives

    Art, music, drama, physical education, and vocational training.
